Privacy Policy for MemeStation

Last updated: 14 December 2025

1. Introduction

MemeStation is an entertainment application that allows users to view memes, unlock special memes using virtual coins, earn daily rewards, and receive extra virtual coins by watching AdMob Reward Ads. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Email Account Login

We collect your display name, profile picture, and email when you sign in using your Email account. This information is used to store your virtual coin balance, unlocked memes, and synchronize data across devices. We do not collect your password.

2.2. App Usage Data

  • Remaining virtual coin balance
  • Reward activity
  • Unlocked content
  • In-app information

This data is stored securely on our server or Supabase and linked to your Email account.

2.3. AdMob Data

We use Google AdMob to serve ads, including Reward Ads. AdMob may collect device information, advertising ID, and ad interaction data. This data is handled by Google under their privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users via Email Sign-in
  • Store virtual coins and unlocked meme status
  • Improve user experience
  • Serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Sync your app data across devices
  • Prevent abuse or cheating

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data except with:

  • Google AdMob – for advertising purposes
  • Supabase – for authentication and data storage
  • Github – for data storage

5. User Rights

You may request data deletion or correction via our contact email. You may also manage Google ad personalization settings at https://www.google.com/settings/ads

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard methods to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption. However, no method is 100% secure.

7. Children

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted here.
